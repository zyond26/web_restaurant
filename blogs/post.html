The Most "Hunted" Imported Beef Series In VietNam

Imported Beef Series In VietNam

Today, as people's quality of life is improving, people are more concerned about health than ever. The factor that directly affects human health is the food source, so imported meat products are increasingly popular. So in Vietnam are there any imported beef lines that are "sought after" and loved? Let's find out with Fresh Foods.

Imported Japanese beef

Wagyu beef: Wagyu beef, also known as "Japanese beef", is a type of purebred beef taken from 4 breeds of cattle including: Black cattle, Hornless cattle, Brown cattle, Short-horned cattle.

Japanese Wagyu beef farms are strictly controlled by breeding processes. Each born cow is named, clearly recording the date and place of birth for periodic health checks. In particular, right from when they were small, the calves were separated to be fed a special diet made from premium hay, rice and pure clean water to ensure the meat quality of purebred Wagyu cows.

Imported American beef

American beef is a famous dish around the world and very popular in Vietnam. American cows are mainly cared for and raised in Northern Scotland, USA. Thanks to favorable climatic conditions and special breeding technology, American cows are fed a nutritious diet with lots of grains, thereby giving the beef a delicious, naturally sweet and fatty flavor. Although there is a lot of fat, American beef does not contain trans fats Monosodium Glutamate and Transfat, on the contrary, it is rich in Vitamins and Protein.

A lot of American beef is imported into Vietnam, however, not all units can provide excellent quality beef products. At T&P Fresh Foods, imported American beef is carefully selected from leading reputable suppliers to ensure the meat is always of the highest quality. With a variety of cuts such as: Tenderloin, sirloin, neck tenderloin, shoulder core, pork belly, ribs, baby back ribs, back lean, neck lean, turtle corn on the cob, beef jerky... all cuts are of high quality. premium quality.

Imported Australian beef

In terms of consumption volume, imported Australian beef always dominates the Vietnamese market. Because the quality is stable, it can be said to be equivalent to American beef, but the price is very affordable, suitable for the average income of Vietnamese people.

Australian cows are raised on farms in Queensland, Australia - a location that helps Australia become the country with the world's leading beef agriculture. Australian beef is always guaranteed the best beef quality right from the time it is raised, thanks to the application of strict breeding technology to produce premium meat and export it around the world, including Vietnam. Male. Similar to other countries in the US and Japan, the most popular Australian beef imported into our country is Wagyu.
